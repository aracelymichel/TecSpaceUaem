EarWat

It is a mobile application that teaches and helps children - young people to understand the importance of water, its cycle and where this critical resource is located, with the main objective of raising awareness about its use, availability and proposals to solve this problem. It uses innovative technology with augmented reality, it is interactive, because the user can navigate through the different modules that make it up and learn from each of them, it is accessible because, in addition to being free, it adapts to different types of learning.


FEATURES

MODULES

Water Cycle

The "Water Cycle" module of our mobile application uses augmented reality to create an interactive environment in which users can explore and understand the water cycle. Using stunning 3D graphics, realistic sound effects and tactile interaction, users can see how water evaporates, condenses, precipitates and flows, while audio assets and on-screen clicks provide them with detailed information about each stage. In addition, gamified challenges are included to encourage learning and awareness of the importance of water and its conservation.

Warming global

The "Global Warming" module of our mobile application offers an interactive and visual educational experience that allows children and youth to explore the evolution of climate change on each continent from 2002 to 2022. Through audio clips with interactive images, users can understand how climate change has affected different regions, identify zones with extreme droughts and wetter areas, and visualize the impact on geography. This free and accessible tool aims to raise awareness of the importance of climate change and promote solutions to this global challenge.

Solutions

The "Solutions" module of our mobile app is an essential part of our educational platform, designed to empower children and youth by providing them with key information on how to contribute to water conservation and climate change mitigation. Through augmented reality technology, users can explore practical and innovative solutions, from water conservation tips to sustainable technologies and community involvement. In addition, success stories and interactive challenges are highlighted to motivate action. This module provides users with the tools and knowledge necessary to become agents of positive change in relation to water and the environment, promoting a more sustainable future.
